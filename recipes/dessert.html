Leche Flan

Eat alone or combined with other desserts

A classic Filipino dessert. Leche Flan is multi-purpose. It can be a standalone dessert or be eaten with other Filipino classic desserts as a topic or on the side.

  • 10 egg yolks
  • 244 g evaporated milk
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  1. Separate the egg yolks and place them in a big bowl and beat using a fork
  2. Add half of the sugar to the yolks and mix well
  3. Add the evaporated milk and vanilla
  4. Caramelize some sugar until it is golden brown and pour it into the mold (the traditional way is using a metal mold, which can be placed directly on the fire and the sugar gets caramelized directly on the mold)
  5. Spread the caramel evenly on the mold, if there are leftover granulated sugar add it to the egg, milk, and vanilla mixture
  6. Once the caramel hardens, pour the rest of the mixture on top
  7. Cover the mold with aluminum foil
  8. Steam the flan mixture for about 30-35 minutes
  9. Allow to cool then refrigerate
  10. Enjoy!
