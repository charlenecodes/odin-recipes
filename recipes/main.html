Sizzling Sisig
It's too good to share
This classic dish originated in Pampanga during the American occupation of the Philippines. Since then it has been popularized to Filipinos across the Philippines and the world.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g pork belly, sliced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 chili peppers or 1/4 tbsp. chili flakes
  • 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1/2 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1/8 ground pepper
  • a pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Season the pork belly with sal and pepper and marinate for at least 30 minutes
  2. Grill the pork belly until it is well done (the skin should be crispy)
  3. Chop the pork belly into small pieces
  4. Combine the chopped meat with onions, lemon juice, chili, then season with soy sauce and pepper
  5. Get a sizzling plate and add some butter, then add the sisig
